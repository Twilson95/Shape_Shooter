Shape Shooter Privacy Policy

Introduction

Our game Shape Shooter respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Our game uses ironSource adverts which collect your general regional location to display relevant ads. We do not collect or store this information; it is used directly by ironSource to provide you with location-based advertisements.

We use Google Play Services for achievements and in-app purchases. When you sign in to your Google Play account through our game, Google shares certain information with us according to your account settings.

Your Consent

By using our game, you consent to our privacy policy.

Changes to this Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page.
